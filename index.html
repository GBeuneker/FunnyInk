Who am I?

I'm Judith van der Stouw; I create illustrations and sell them on products such as postcards. I primarily draw animals that look mythical but are real, with ink pen. I provide fun and interesting facts about them alongside the illustrations in a column in Gay News magazine. I am currently creating illustrations for a children's book project that I'm part of.
